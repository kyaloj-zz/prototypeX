Features

Daily Prices

Monitor the daily market price performance of your investment using the daily prices feature. See the value gain or loss your crop has experienced daily and watch out for those which are performing better or worse on our top 5 daily gainers and losers.

Trends

Before investing into or pulling out of a crop investment, use this feature to monitor its performance for the past 2 to 6 upto 12 seasons and understand the price raise or drop points and use this feature to determine which crop to invest in, at what time of the year, and for what market..
