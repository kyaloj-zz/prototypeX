To pay with Mpesa do the following:

  1. Go to "M-Pesa" menu on your phone
  2. Select "Pay Bill"
  3. Enter 967701 as the business number
  4. Enter 245 as the account no.
  5. Enter the amount to pay
  6. Enter your M-Pesa pin
  7. Then send the request
Kindly note that it will take up to 1 hour from payment before you can access the prices Any problems with this procedure then contact M-Farm on phone: 0707933993
